Explore Vancouver Island

Adventures from Mount Washington to Victoria, the capital city.

Two Locations, Endless Adventures

Vancouver Island is a year-round destination, offering a rare blend of mountains and city charm. From winter powder days to vibrant summer festivals, we've curated our favorite seasonal experiences to help you plan your perfect stay.

The Mountain Experience

Top 5 Summer Highlights

Lift-Accessed Mountain Biking

Experience the thrill of downhill biking at Mount Washington's renowned bike park.

Hike in Strathcona Park

Discover pristine lakes and stunning vistas in BC's oldest provincial park.

Explore Cumberland Village

Visit the historic village known for its legendary biking trails and craft brewery.

Swim at Comox Lake

A popular spot for swimming, boating, and picnicking on a warm summer day.

Visit a Farmers' Market

Experience the local bounty with fresh produce and artisan goods in the Comox Valley.

Top 5 Winter Highlights

Alpine Skiing & Snowboarding

Hit the slopes at Mount Washington, known for its deep powder and ocean views.

Cross-Country Skiing

Explore 55km of groomed trails that wind through serene alpine forests.

Snowshoeing Adventures

Trek through a winter wonderland on a variety of marked trails for all skill levels.

Fat Biking in the Snow

Try a unique winter sport on the snowy trails around Cumberland.

Stargazing in the Alpine

On clear nights, the minimal light pollution offers breathtaking views of the stars.

The Capital City Experience

Top 5 Summer Highlights

Explore the Inner Harbour

The vibrant heart of the city, perfect for whale watching tours, street performers, and festivals.

Cycle the Galloping Goose Trail

Rent bikes and explore this scenic trail that runs from Victoria into the countryside.

Visit The Butchart Gardens

Experience the world-renowned floral displays, especially vibrant in the summer.

Discover Fisherman's Wharf

A unique marine destination with colourful float homes, fresh seafood, and friendly seals.

Hike in Mount Douglas Park

Climb to the summit for a 360-degree view of the city, ocean, and islands.

Top 5 Winter Highlights

Visit the Royal BC Museum

Immerse yourself in the natural and human history of British Columbia, a perfect rainy day activity.

Christmas at Butchart Gardens

Experience the Magic of Christmas with spectacular light displays and festive entertainment.

Wander Through Fan Tan Alley

Explore Canada's narrowest street in the heart of the country's oldest Chinatown.

Enjoy Afternoon Tea

Indulge in a classic Victoria tradition at one of the city's many charming tea rooms.

Discover Sidney-by-the-Sea

Explore the charming seaside town's unique bookstores and waterfront walkway.
